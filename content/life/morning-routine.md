A simple morning routine that sets the tone for the entire day.

## The Routine

1. **Hydrate** - Glass of water before anything else
2. **Move** - 2 minutes of stretching
3. **Breathe** - 10 deep breaths, focusing on exhale
4. **Intention** - One sentence: "Today I will..."
5. **Start** - Begin the most important task immediately

## Why It Works

The key insight: **momentum compounds**. Starting small removes friction. The hardest part is getting out of bed; everything after that is easier.

## Tracking

I use a simple text file:

```
2026-01-11: intention="finish TIL site" / completed=yes
2026-01-10: intention="review PRs" / completed=partial
```

No apps, no complexity. Just words.

> "How you do anything is how you do everything."

The morning sets the pattern.
