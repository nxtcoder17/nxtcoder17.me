Starting a TIL habit is about more than just documenting knowledge - it's about building a mindset of continuous growth.

## Why Daily Learning Matters

- **Compounding** - Small learnings add up over time
- **Reflection** - Writing forces you to understand deeply
- **Reference** - Your future self will thank you

## The Process

1. Notice something new or interesting
2. Take a moment to understand it
3. Write it down in your own words
4. Share it (optional but powerful)

> "The best time to plant a tree was 20 years ago. The second best time is now."

Starting small is fine. Even one sentence counts. The habit matters more than the volume.

## Making It Stick

- Keep it simple - don't overcomplicate the format
- Be consistent - same time each day helps
- Review periodically - look back at past months
