title: Building a sustainable reading habit
date: 2026-01-09
---

After years of failed attempts, I finally cracked the reading habit code.

## The Mistake

I used to set ambitious goals: "Read 52 books this year!" Then I'd burn out by February.

## The Fix

**Read just 2 pages before bed.** That's it.

The magic is in the constraint:
- 2 pages is so small you can't say no
- It happens at the same time daily (habit stacking)
- Most nights, you'll read more than 2 pages
- But you never *have* to

## Results

- 2024: 4 books (old approach)
- 2025: 18 books (2-page rule)

## Books That Changed My Thinking

1. *Atomic Habits* - James Clear (meta, I know)
2. *The Mom Test* - Rob Fitzpatrick
3. *Staff Engineer* - Will Larson

The goal isn't to read more. It's to *become* a reader. Identity precedes behavior.
